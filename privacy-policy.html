Privacy Policy for Splitstorm

Effective Date: April 5th, 2025.

At Splitstorm, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Splitstorm may collect certain types of information, including but not limited to:

  • User-generated data such as names, event times, and race information
  • E-mails, passwords, or other information used in account creation
  • Device data (e.g., device model, operating system version)
  • Usage data to improve app performance and user experience

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Operate and improve the Splitstorm app
  • Store and display race and timing records in our servers
  • Support real-time syncing between devices (if logged in)

3. No Data Sharing with Advertisers or Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share any of your personal information with advertisers or third parties. Your data stays private and is only used for the core functionality of the Splitstorm app.

4. Offline Functionality

Splitstorm is designed to work offline. All race data and results are stored locally on your device unless you opt-in to use syncing features or backups.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or loss. However, no method of digital storage or transmission is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by other means.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at:
