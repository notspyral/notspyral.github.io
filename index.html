Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your Splitstorm account, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the Teams tab on the navigation bar.
  3. Tap the Menu button on the top-right of the screen.
  4. Click on the "Delete Account" button.
  5. Confirm your decision by following the on-screen instructions.

Once your account is deleted, all associated data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
